Why Cycling Matters

Why Cycle to Work?

Eco-Friendly

Cycling produces zero emissions, helping to reduce air pollution and combat climate change. Each mile cycled saves approximately 1 pound of CO2 compared to driving.

Health Benefits

Regular cycling improves cardiovascular health, builds muscle strength, and reduces stress. Commuters who cycle regularly have 40% lower risk of premature death.

Cost Effective

Save thousands annually on fuel, parking, and vehicle maintenance. The average annual cost of owning and operating a bicycle is just 1% of the cost of a car.
